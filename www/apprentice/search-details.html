The apprenticeship will involve learning to operate a variety of machinery such as milling machines, surface grinders and bandsaws.

The successful candidate will also be required to manufacture parts using raw materials from engineering drawings, sketches or verbal instructions.

Further duties include:

  • checking parts using measuring equipment
  • recording process times
  • reporting defects
  • planning process methods
  • ensuring Health and Safety requirements are adhered to

The apprenticeship is subject to a mandatory 3-month probationary period. There will also be a 1-day trial prior to an offer.

Requirements and prospects

Desired skills

  • A high standard of maths and IT.
  • Excellent communication skills.
  • Confidential approach on sensitive projects

Qualifications required

GSCE grade A-C in Mathematics and English. (or equivalent)

Future prospects

Advancement to Level 3 qualification is encouraged and supported.

On successful completion of the apprenticeship, there will be a full-time position available, leading to competitive salary in excess of £28k + benefits.

Things to consider

  • You must be capable of standing during all working hours.
  • A degree of physical work will be required.

About the employer

Established in 1978, R G Engineering Ltd is proud to have developed partnerships with leading companies that include Rolls-Royce Aero Engines, Denso, and GKN Aerospace. We supply design and manufacture products and onsite customer support to the aerospace, automotive, and robotic markets.

Being a medium company we are able to dedicate ourselves to 100% customer focus, delivering high quality parts in short lead-time’s without compromise.

Important Other Information

The National Minimum Wage (NMW) for Apprentices is £2.68 per hour from October 2013. This applies to 16-18 year old Apprentices and those aged over 19 in the first year of their Apprenticeship. For all other Apprentices the National Minimum wage appropriate to their age applies. The Minimum Wage for Apprentices applies to both time spent on the job plus time spent training.
