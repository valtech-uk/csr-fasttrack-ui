Why we collect this info

Employers who are part of the ‘Positive about disabled people’ scheme will guarantee candidates an interview who meet the apprenticeship criteria if they have a disability.

For this reason we ask you to provide this information so we can inform the employer and provider.

What is your ethnic group?

We need this information to make sure we're giving equal opportunities to all candidates

Why are we asking all these questions?

The Skills Funding Agency aims to make apprenticeships and traineeships inclusive. Learning more about you will help us do this and meet our obligations under the Equality Act 2010.

You don’t have to answer the questions, but the information allows us to make the application process easier.

Your answers will be treated in confidence and are covered by the Data Protection Act 1998. We’ll only share them with recruiters if you grant us permission.

Answering the questions may lead to a guaranteed interview if you are disabled and the employer is a member of the Two Ticks scheme.
